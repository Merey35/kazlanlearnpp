Сұраулы сөйлемдер (Interrogative Sentences)

In Kazakh, interrogative sentences are used when you want to ask questions, just like in English.
One of the first and most common questions is: «Бұл не?», which means “What is this?”

Let’s see how it works

Kazakh English Example Answer
Бұл не? What is this? Бұл кітап. → This is a book.
Бұл не? What is this? Бұл алма. → This is an apple.

Explanation

Бұл (Bul) = “this”
не (ne) = “what”
So literally, “This what?” — but in Kazakh, that’s the correct word order for a question.

People: use «кім» instead of «не»

If you’re talking about a person, you can’t say «Бұл не?» because that asks “What is this?” Instead use «Бұл кім?» — “Who is this?”

Kazakh English Example
Бұл кім? Who is this? Бұл мұғалім.
This is a teacher.
Бұл кім? Who is this? Бұл менің ағам.
This is my brother.

Other popular question words

Kazakh English Used for
Не? What? Things
Кім? Who? People
Қайда? Where? Place
Қашан? When? Time
Қанша? / Неше? How many? Numbers, quantity

Question intonation and writing

In speech: your tone usually rises at the end: «Бұл не?».
In writing: use the question mark ? at the end.
