Есімдіктер және жіктік жалғаулар (Pronouns and personal endings)

Do you want to learn other ways of saying who you or someone else are in Kazakh?
To do that, you’ll need to know two important things: pronouns and personal endings.
They help you talk about yourself, introduce others, and have simple conversations.

Pronouns (Есімдіктер)

Pronouns replace names and tell who you’re talking about.
In Kazakh, pronouns don’t change by gender, e.g. “he” and “she” are the same word “ол”.

English Kazakh Example
I Мен Мен оқушымын - I am a student
You (informal) Сен Сен кімсің? - Who are you?
You (formal) Сіз Сіз мұғалімсіз бе? - Are you a teacher?
He / She Ол Ол досым - He/She is my friend
We Біз Біз қазақпыз - We are Kazakh
You (plural) Сендер / Сіздер Сендер оқушысыңдар - You (all) are students
They Олар Олар дәрігерлер - They are doctors

Personal Endings (Жіктік жалғаулар)

In Kazakh, personal endings are added to the end of a word (usually a verb or noun) to show who it refers to.
This is how you say things like “I am”, “You are”, “He is”, etc.
However, each pronoun can have several endings:

Pronoun (English) Pronoun (Kazakh) Common Personal Endings
I Мен -мын / -мін / -бын / -бін / -пын / -пін
You (informal singular) Сен -сың / -сің
You (formal singular) Сіз -сыз / -сіз
He / She / It Ол (no ending)
We Біз -мыз / -міз / -быз / -біз / -пыз / -піз
You (plural/formal) Сендер / Сіздер -сыңдар / -сіңдер / -сыздар / -сіздер
They Олар (plural ending(-лар / -лер / -дар / -дер / -тар / -тер))

Endings change according to vowel harmony:

  • If the word has а, о, ұ, ы → use “hard” endings (e.g. -мын, -сың, -мыз)
  • If the word has ә, ө, ү, і → use “soft” endings (e.g. -мін, -сің, -міз)

Example with the word “оқушы” (student)

Person Pronoun Word + Ending Translation
1st singular Мен оқушымын I am a student
2nd singular (informal) Сен оқушысың You are a student
2nd singular (formal) Сіз оқушысыз You are a student (formal)
3rd singular Ол оқушы He/She is a student
1st plural Біз оқушымыз We are students
2nd plural (informal) Сендер оқушысыңдар You (all) are students
2nd plural (formal) Сіздер оқушысыздар You (all) are students (formal)
3rd plural Олар оқушылар They are students
